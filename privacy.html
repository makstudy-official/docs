Privacy Policy

Effective as of August 25, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the MAKstudy app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Anish Dandapat (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service. MAKstudy is an unofficial educational app designed for students of Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), providing access to notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), lecture materials, and books to support engineering studies. The Application is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to MAKAUT. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The MAKstudy app does not collect or store any personal information from users, including but not limited to your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, usage data, or personally identifiable information such as email, name, or profile image. The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Third-Party Access

The MAKstudy app does not share personal information with third parties because no personal information is collected. However, the Application utilizes third-party services to provide advertisements, host content, and manage PDF links. These services may collect data as described in their own Privacy Policies. Below are the third-party services used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Cloudinary
  • Supabase

The Service Provider may disclose information as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, but since no personal data is collected, such disclosures are not applicable to user data.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop any potential data collection by third-party services by uninstalling the MAKstudy app. You may use the standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace.

Data Retention Policy

Since the MAKstudy app does not collect personal information, no user data is retained by the Service Provider.

Children

The MAKstudy app does not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that a child has interacted with the app, please contact the Service Provider at playconsoleanish@gmail.com.

Security

Since no personal information is collected by the MAKstudy app, no specific security measures are required for user data. The Service Provider relies on third-party services (e.g., Cloudinary, Supabase) to handle content securely, and you should review their Privacy Policies for details on their security practices.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page or within the MAKstudy app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the MAKstudy app, you consent to this Privacy Policy as set forth by the Service Provider, Anish Dandapat.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the MAKstudy app, or have questions about the practices, please contact the Service Provider, Anish Dandapat, via email at playconsoleanish@gmail.com.
