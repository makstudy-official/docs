Welcome to MAKstudy

MAKstudy is an unofficial educational app designed for students of Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT). It provides access to notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), lecture materials, and books to support engineering studies.

Important Note: The app is developed by Anish Dandapat and is provided as a free service, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to MAKAUT.

Study Materials

MAKstudy provides everything you need in one place — semester-wise notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), video lectures, organizers, and recommended books. Stay organized and study smarter with resources curated specially for MAKAUT students.
